Resources

Mobilizing shared methods, tools, and resources across our network

Country and Regional Databases

Detailed information on known non-native species occurrences, ecology, and invasion impacts.

NEMESIS

National Exotic Marine and Estuarine Species Information System

Marine and estuarine invertebrates and algae introduced to the continental United States and Alaska.

CalNEMO

California Non-native Estuarine and Marine Organisms

Marine and estuarine invertebrates and algae introduced to the U.S. state of California; incorporates occurrence data from the CANOD inventory of non-native species in California.

GalNEMO

Galápagos Non-native Estuarine and Marine Organisms

Marine and estuarine invertebrates and algae introduced to the Galápagos.

PanNEMO

Panamá Non-native Estuarine and Marine Organisms

Marine and estuarine invertebrates and algae introduced to Panamá.

Standardized Protocols for Detecting Bioinvasions

Alert System for Regional Invasions

We are working to develop alerting systems to help resource managers increase their awareness of species newly reported from adjacent or similar areas and target detection and response effots.

Community Engagement & Outreach

COMBINA aims to highlight resources for successful engagement with local communities to build awareness and partnerships around locally and regionally relevant marine biosecurity issues.
